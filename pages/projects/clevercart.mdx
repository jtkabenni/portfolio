### Features

- **Authenticate:** Log in securely with your Google account.
- **Create and share lists**: Create new shopping lists and share them with other CleverCart users.
- **Add, delete, search for items**: Add new items to shopping lists, purchase items, and delete items.
- **Smart suggestions**: The list will display an urgency status based on previous purchase behavior.

Parktrippers lets you create the perfect itinerary for a trip to a US national park. With Parktrippers, you can select a park, browse the events and activities that are available during the dates of your stay, and add them to a daily or multi-day itinerary. The app has backend functionality allowing users to create accounts and save trips to the profiles.
